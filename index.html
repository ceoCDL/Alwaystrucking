Industry-Leading Training Programs

Choose from our comprehensive selection of courses designed to help you start or advance your trucking career with skills that employers demand.

CDL Continuing Education

Stay current with regulations, safety, and industry best practices for experienced drivers. Meets all FMCSA requirements for continuing education.

Dispatcher Training

Learn load planning, communication, TMS software, and regulatory compliance for logistics staff. Average salary increase of 35% after completion.

Hours of Service Compliance

Master federal and state hours-of-service rules, ELD use, and proper recordkeeping. Prevent violations and maintain CSA score excellence.

Vehicle Inspection & Maintenance

Focus on pre-trip, en-route, and post-trip inspections plus basic maintenance. Reduce roadside issues by up to 75% with proper training.

Defensive Driving

Learn safe driving techniques, hazard perception, and accident prevention strategies. Carriers report 40% fewer incidents from our graduates.

TMS Training

Hands-on instruction for using modern Transportation Management System software. Master the tools used by the top logistics companies nationwide.
